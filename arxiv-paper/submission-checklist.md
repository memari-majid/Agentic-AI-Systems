## Common Issues to Avoid

### LaTeX Compilation
- ❌ Using custom packages not on arXiv
- ❌ Absolute file paths
- ❌ Missing figure files
- ❌ Using system-specific fonts

### Content
- ❌ Plagiarism or insufficient attribution
- ❌ Low-quality or irrelevant submissions
- ❌ Duplicate submissions
- ❌ Non-scientific content

### Formatting
- ❌ Abstract > 1920 characters
- ❌ Figures in wrong format
- ❌ Broken references
- ❌ Missing bibliography

## Tips for Success

1. **Test Compilation**: Compile your paper multiple times on a clean system
2. **Use Standard Packages**: Stick to arXiv-approved LaTeX packages
3. **Check References**: Verify all citations are complete and accurate
4. **Quality Figures**: Use vector graphics (PDF, EPS) when possible
5. **Clear Writing**: Have colleagues review for clarity
6. **Follow Guidelines**: Read arXiv submission guidelines carefully
7. **Version Control**: Keep track of all versions and changes
